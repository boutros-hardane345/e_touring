Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about volunteering

Do I need previous experience?

No experience necessary! We provide comprehensive training for all volunteers. We believe enthusiasm and willingness to learn are more important than prior experience. Our team will guide you through everything you need to know.

What's the minimum time commitment?

We offer flexible volunteering opportunities. You can volunteer as little as 4 hours per month or join us full-time. We'll work with your schedule to find a commitment level that suits you.

Is there an age requirement?

Volunteers must be 18 or older for most positions. Minors (16-17) can participate with parental consent and supervision. We also offer family-friendly events where younger volunteers can participate with their parents.

Do you provide equipment?

We provide necessary equipment for most activities including gloves, trash bags, and tools. For hiking events, you'll need your own hiking shoes and weather-appropriate clothing. We'll send you a detailed list of what to bring before each event.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We have remote opportunities in content creation, social media management, graphic design, and research. Let us know your skills and interests, and we'll find a role that fits.
