Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an event?

You can book events directly through our website by visiting the Events page and clicking "Register" on your chosen event. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your booking.

What should I bring to a hike?

We recommend bringing: comfortable hiking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, water bottle, snacks, sun protection, and a small backpack. We'll send you a detailed list after booking.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel up to 48 hours before the event for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may receive partial refund or credit for future events. Check our cancellation policy for details.

Are your events suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer events for all skill levels. Each event listing includes a difficulty rating (Easy, Medium, Hard) to help you choose the right experience for your fitness level.
