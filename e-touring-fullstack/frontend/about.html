About e_Touring

Connecting people with nature through sustainable eco-tourism

Who We Are

e_Touring is a pioneering eco-tourism initiative based in Lebanon, dedicated to promoting sustainable travel experiences that respect and preserve our natural environment. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small group of nature enthusiasts to a community of over 1000 members who share a common passion for Lebanon's breathtaking landscapes.

We believe that tourism can be a force for good – protecting nature, supporting local communities, and educating people about the importance of environmental conservation. Every tour, event, and activity we organize is designed with sustainability at its core.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable eco-adventures that inspire people to connect with nature, while actively contributing to the preservation of Lebanon's natural heritage through responsible tourism practices and community engagement.

Our Vision

To become Lebanon's leading eco-tourism platform, creating a network of protected natural areas and empowered local communities, where sustainable tourism serves as a model for environmental conservation and economic development.
